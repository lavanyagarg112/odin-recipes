Spring Roll

Description

Spring rolls are a fantastic choice for a crispy, vibrant dish. Here's a recipe that encapsulates the essence of spring in every bite!

Ingredients

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Firm tofu, drained and crumbled
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Finely chopped carrots
  • Thinly sliced mushrooms
  • Chopped onions
  • Minced garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Egg or water (for sealing the wrappers)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare the Tofu: Press the tofu to remove excess water, then crumble it into small pieces.
  2. Sauté the Vegetables and Tofu: In a pan, heat a little oil and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant. Add the crumbled tofu, carrots, mushrooms, and cabbage. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  3. Season the Filling: Add soy sauce and sesame oil to the vegetable mixture. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the filling cool down before assembling.
  4. Wrap the Spring Rolls: Place a wrapper on a flat surface, add a spoonful of the filling to the center, fold in the sides, then roll tightly. Use a little water or beaten egg to seal the end.
  5. Fry the Spring Rolls: Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan. Fry the spring rolls in batches until they're golden brown and crispy. Don't overcrowd the pan to ensure even cooking.
  6. Drain Excess Oil: Once fried, transfer the spring rolls to a plate lined with paper towels to soak up any excess oil.
  7. Serve: Enjoy the spring rolls hot with your choice of dipping sauce, such as sweet and sour sauce or a homemade dipping sauce made with soy sauce, rice vinegar, and a touch of chili oil for some heat.
